Tip #1- Stay On The Latest Firmware

Companies (basically Apple and Google) take a while to shoot out an update for all of their devices. Depending if there are new features being implemented or just theres not a point. Whatever the reason is, stay on the latest firmware, which goes for any type of device (PCs, Phones, Tablets etc). The reason behind all this is because there are always breaches in the system. Hackers find ways to steal your information in any possible way. Which is why manufacturers or comapanies patch the hole up with updates.

Tip #2- Have A Strong Password!!!

Sure, shorter and less complex passwords are better to remember, but keep in mind people want what you have. If your bank account password is "1234567890" or "passw0rd", consider your account breached and obtained by someone else that could be 5 hrs away or posssibly your neighbor.

Tip #4- Check The Website

Make sure ou information isn't leaked while you are browing the web. To check look in the top left corner of your screen to see if there is a green "HTTPS" in the front of the url code for the given website. If the "S" is not there you should proceed with caution or et off acnd find another website with the same information.
